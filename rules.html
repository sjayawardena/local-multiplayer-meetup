Our Rules

  • - Children must be accompanied by an adult.
  • - None of the consoles are connected to the internet, and connecting them should not be attempted. The meet up is to play local multiplayer only.
  • - Please spend a maximum of 30 minutes on any console station, so all have a chance to play what they want.
  • - Let others join your multiplayer session at the first possible opportunity.
  • - Please be gentle with all consoles, accessories, equipment etc. In some cases due to their age, they are quite rare.
  • - Please do not adjust the volume at any of the gaming stations. They have been set so everybody will be able to hear what they are playing.
  • - Be welcoming to others and treat them with respect. The meetup is for everyone.
  • - Have fun and express yourself - but please no swearing.
  • - If somebody prefers not to play with you, please accept this and move on- they have their reasons.
  • - You may bring food and drink, but no alcohol. And please take your litter away with you.
