Welcome!

We meet up once a week and play multiplayer video games as you remember them: in person!

All levels of gamers are welcome - from the curious, to the casual, to the committed.

We have various gaming stations for up to 4 players with current generation consoles, as well as classic consoles (Nintendo, Sega, and more!).

All are well-stocked with a selection of games that are easy to turn on and play in local multiplayer mode.
