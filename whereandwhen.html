Where and When

You can join us on Saturdays, any time between 12pm and 5pm at the Sandbunker Community Centre in Noel Park, N22, North London.

Stay for as long as you like, play as many games as you like and, most importantly, make friends along the way!

We charge £5 entry for adults, £2.50 for kids.

Any questions? Please email: nlondon-multiplayer-meetup@gmail.com
